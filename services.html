    Energy Conservation Audits


    Our team consists of Energy Auditors and Energy Managers who are certified by BEE ( Bureau of Energy Efficiency ,under Ministry of Power , Government of India). Our auditors and managers have a rich experience of 25 years in the field of energy conservation. Thermal and Electrical related loads are audited under the conservation audit. We have been regularly conducting our audits for segments and industries such as Engineering , Pharma , Textile , Sugar ,Medical , Cement , Paper , Food processing and solvent extraction , Commercial buildings, Dairy etc ..

    The team is equipped with sophesticated testing equipments such as Power analyzer , Flure gas analyser , Thermography , Water flow meter , Hygrometer , Anemometer , manoometer ,Lux meter etc. We provide our technical assistance during implementation phase as well, after submission of audit report.
